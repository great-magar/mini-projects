Performance Scholarships

The College awards full scholarships (Semester Fees waiver) to students who achieve an SGPA of 4.0 in any Semester for the subsequent Semester. PHOENIX COLLEGE also awards those students who are class toppers or second- toppers.

Admission Process

Students interested in joining BE at PHOENIX COLLEGE would need to go through a fair but thorough admission process.

Eligibility

Applicants must have the following minimum levels in +2 or equivalent exams.

Engineering: Applicants need at least Second Division (45%) in +2/equivalent exams or minimum C grade in each subject in XI and XII separately.

Application Form

PHOENIX COLLEGE Entrance Exam Application forms are available. Applicants should submit these forms duly filled in with supporting documents and photographs by the given deadline.

Entrance Exam

Getting through the Exam is mandatory for BE since the objective of the PHOENIX COLLEGE admission policy is to select students on a competitive basis. Questions asked are from related subject areas. Sample Test Papers can be obtained from PHOENIX COLLEGE along with Application forms.

Enrollment

Once the College receives a letter of acceptance, it confirms admission forthwith subject to the payment of fees.
